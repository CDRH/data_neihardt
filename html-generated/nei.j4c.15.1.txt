                <span class="salute">Dear Comrade:</span>
            
                <p>X (   Please apply at once for copyright in my name.</p>
                
                <p>This is, of course, only the preparation of the background for the tragedy.</p>
                
                <p>The theme of the Song of the Messiah is this: Spiritual values triumph in this world only through defeat.</p>
                
                <p>A corollary to the proposition, proved over and over in history is: Whenever spiritual values appear to triumph through worldly victory, only an empty form remains.</p>
                
                <p>The Oglalas are preparing for our spring pow-wows. Much evidence of intense interest up there. Can hardly wait until spring - want to go now. It will be a wonderful experience, for I can feel that everything is shaping up just as I would have it.</p>
                
                <span class="closer">
                <span class="salute">Endless love,</span>
                <br/>Jno.
                </span>
                
                <p>Be <u>very</u> careful with proofs, &amp; if at all possible let me read proofs too.</p>
                <span class="closer">
         <br/>-J.</span>
