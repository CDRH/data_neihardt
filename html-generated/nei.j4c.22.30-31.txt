            <h4>WHAT IS CHRISTMAS?</h4> 


                
            <span class="lg">
               <span class="l">This holiest of all the nights,</span>                             
               <span class="l">I wonder what it means?</span>                                     
               <span class="l">It's surely more than candlelights</span>                          
               <span class="l">On tinselled evergreens.</span>                                    
               <span class="l">It's more than toys that make it dear</span>                       
               <span class="l">And eating pleasant things, </span>                                 
               <span class="l">For if you listen right you'll hear </span>                        
               <span class="l">A murmuring of wings.</span>
            </span>                                   
                
            <span class="lg">
               <span class="l">My grandma says its more than fun</span>         
               <span class="l">And hanging up your stocking.</span>
               <span class="l">It's knowing any needy one</span>
               <span class="l">Might be the Savior knocking.</span>
               <span class="l">It's helping those who feel the rod</span>
               <span class="l">Of grief and heavy labors.</span> 
               <span class="l">I guess its being nice to God</span>
               <span class="l">By loving all your neighbors</span>
            </span>

            <span class="byline">by John Neihardt</span>
