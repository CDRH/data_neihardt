                <span class="salute">Dear Dr. Kendrick:</span>
                
                <p>Many, many thanks for the <u>Book</u>! And what a book it is! I'm reading with real pleasure and profit, for I know you are master of the subject. I admire the way in which you have organized your knowledge, and the clarity with which you present it.</p> 
                
                <p>Some months ago you sent me a beautiful little essay of yours, and I didn't say a word to you about it. I'm ashamed, but I'm thanking you now. There's a poet

                    in you certainly, but that did not surprise me after my visit with you and your brother.</p>
                
                <p>I'm sending you my latest — about the Sioux; and I'm getting the better of the trade!</p>
                
                <p>Thanks again — and kindest thoughts!</p>
