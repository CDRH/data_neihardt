   <h3>
      <a class="persNameLink" href="/doc/vandyke.h">Van Dyke, Henry, 1852-1933.</a>
   </h3>
   <p>
      <div class="note">American writer, educator, born in Pennsylvania, educated at Princeton and Princeton Theological Seminary; taught as professor literature at Princeton (1899-1923).  An ordained minister, he chaired the committee that wrote the Presbyterian <em class="hi">Book of Common Worship</em> and wrote hundreds of poems, essays, sermons, hymns, and short stories, including “The First Christmas Tree.”  His Romantic and natural theological approach to human life and its sympathetic relationship with nature influenced his aesthetic taste and literary criticism.</div>
