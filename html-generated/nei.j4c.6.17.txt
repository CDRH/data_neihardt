      <span class="dateline">Bancroft, Aug. 18, '20.</span>

      <span class="salute">Dear Sterling: —</span>


	     <p>A little while ago Mr. &amp; Mrs. Ralph Waldo Trine visiting here with us. Mrs. T. is a booster of poets, and I gave a letter of introduction to you.</p>
	     <p>That was a good letter from you, and I had a good chuckle over the "two murders and a rope"; but I knew damned well that the drama would be a gem. When am I to see it, Old Man?</p>
