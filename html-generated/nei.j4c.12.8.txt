      <span class="dateline">Feb. 27 '27</span>

      <span class="salute">Dearest Comrade:</span>

      <p>Don't worry about this moment in a silly but necessary social experiment. Of course the rabble isn't educable.</p>

      <p>We are doing something - you and I - that will outlast this twilight.</p>

      <p>Signs of the growing success of the scheme are increasing.</p>

      <p>I'm glad you're in the world with me.</p>
      <span class="closer">
         <span class="salute">Endless love,</span>

         <br/>Jno.
