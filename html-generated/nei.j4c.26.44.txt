                <span class="salute">Dear Bower:</span>
                
                <p>As you know, I wrote Lucile that I'd like to visit at your home from about Dec. 26 to around Jan. 1. That is the only free time I can see ahead before next summer, and we have a lot to do, I feel, before Lucile begins writing on the biography. She says for me to come on, that Gramby will be there, but you will be in 
                
                
                
                
                Washington! I wonder, is Fate trying to be funny? Last time you were in the hospital. This time, 
                <u>if</u> I come, are you to be on the other side of the continent?</p>
               
               <p>This is the point of my note: Do you plan to be away <u>all that time</u>? Would I miss you entirely?</p>
                <span class="closer">
                <span class="salute">Affectionately</span>
                <br/>John
                </span>
