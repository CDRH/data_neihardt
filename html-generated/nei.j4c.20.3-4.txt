                <span class="salute">Dear Dr. Kendrick:</span>
                
                <p>Lyndal has heard the story of Mr. Stork and the Pickle Fork five times, and would have heard it more but for the fact that we've been having flu for a week. She says for me to say to you: "Many thanks for the story. I like it very much, and I think it pays to be polite."</p>
                
                <p>It <u>is</u> a good yarn. We have not yet been able to read "The Holy Earth", but we are coming out

                    of the shadow of the flu, &amp; soon we shall read the book aloud. Many, <u>many</u> thanks for wanting to share it with us. You will hear from us.</p>
