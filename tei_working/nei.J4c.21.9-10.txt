<p>Dear Mrs. Aly:</p>

	<p>I talked and read lyrics this forenoon (9-10:30) at Stephens College (about 50 students). It was "one of those things", the special sort that I love when "something takes over". I report this because I understand you want such information for your dissertation. Prof. Charles Madden of Stephens was in charge and would,

-2-

I'm sure, be glad to respond if you cared to question him. You understand I'm only trying to be "helpful"!</p>
